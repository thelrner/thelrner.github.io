Cellular Warfare

Score

Rules

  • Regular snake rules apply: eat apples, avoid the wall and yourself.
  • Enemy cells spawn randomly. Eating an apple spawns friendly cells. Snake can move through cells.
  • Cell propagation obeys Conway's Game with a twist -- friendly cells capture enemy cells by surrounding them.

Scoring

  • Consuming apple - 50 points
  • Capturing enemy cell - 1 point

Controls

  • ↑←↓→ - Move
  • SPACE - Pause
